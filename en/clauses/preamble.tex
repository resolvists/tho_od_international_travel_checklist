\section*{\centering TRAVEL PREPARATION CHECKLIST}
\vspace*{0.5cm}

International travel requires thoughtful planning and careful organization to ensure a seamless experience from departure to return. Important considerations such as passport validity, visa requirements, health precautions, financial arrangements, and packing essentials must be addressed well in advance. A comprehensive travel preparation checklist provides a structured approach to managing these details, minimizing unexpected complications and reducing travel-related stress. By systematically reviewing each requirement before departure, travelers can enhance safety, efficiency, and overall enjoyment throughout the journey.
